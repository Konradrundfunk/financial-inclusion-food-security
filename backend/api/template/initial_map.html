Our Angola projects.

Namibe

Agriculture is the main source of income in Namibe Province, the principal crops being citrus fruits, olives, guava, millet, and livestock, including the rearing of sheep and goats. The region is very mountainous and does not provide good conditions for agriculture.

Huambo

Huambo is the richest agricultural province in Angola. The Province produces almost 22% of total national cereal production. Despite that there is still an large smallholder farmer population with no proper access to financial services.
